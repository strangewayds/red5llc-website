Let's Talk

A Safe Space to Share

A kind, encouraging place to talk, share and connect — always gentle, always welcoming. Need a friend right now? Rosie is always here to listen.

This is a moderated, supportive space — not emergency or therapy support

Let's Talk is reviewed by trained grown-up moderators to keep it kind and safe. Red 5 is a supportive community and educational resource. It is not a replacement for professional therapy, medical care, or emergency support. If a child is in immediate danger, contact emergency services.

How Let's Talk works

A few simple promises that keep everyone comfortable.

  • 1
    Be kind & respectful. We lift each other up — no mean words, teasing or bullying.
  • 2
    A grown-up is always watching. Friendly moderators review messages to keep this space safe.
  • 3
    Keep private things private. Never share your full name, address, school, phone number or passwords.
  • 4
    It's okay to ask for help. If something feels wrong, tell a trusted adult or use the report button.
