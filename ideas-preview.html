Parent FAQ accordion

Answers safety, age and billing questions right where doubt happens. Pure HTML — no JavaScript needed. Membership + Parents.
Is Red 5 really safe for my child?
Yes. Red 5 is ad-free and human-moderated. Chats are watched by real moderators, and there are no strangers, links, or outside messaging.
What ages is it for?
Red 5 is designed for kids roughly ages 5–12, with parents in control of the account. Content is positive, encouraging and age-appropriate.
How is my family's data handled?
We collect the minimum needed to run the account and we never sell your data. Parents can review and delete information any time.
What do I get with the $4.99 Club?
Exclusive videos, unlimited games, the private community, monthly challenges, rewards & badges, and printable activity packs.
Can I cancel any time?
Absolutely. There are no contracts — cancel in one click from your parent dashboard and you won't be billed again.
