Membership questions

Everything a grown-up might want to know before joining.

How much does the Red 5 Club cost?

The Red 5 Club is $4.99 per month, with no surprise fees. There's also a free plan so you can look around together before deciding. One Club membership covers your whole household.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Parents manage the membership and can cancel at any time — there are no long-term contracts and no cancellation fees.

Is there a free option?

Absolutely. Red 5 Free gives you a taste of the Play Zone and Create Zone, sample Rosie videos, story previews, and full access to the parent dashboard — with no ads and no time limit.

Will my child see ads?

No. Red 5 is completely ad-free on both the Free and Club plans, and we never target children with behavioral advertising.

Is it safe? Who controls the account?

A parent or guardian sets up and oversees the account, and the community is human-moderated. Read more in our parent & safety guide. Note: Red 5 is a prototype today — full safety relies on human moderators and a secure backend that go live before launch.

How many kids can use one membership?

A single Club membership is designed to cover the children in your household, all managed from one parent account.

Is my payment information secure?

When the Club launches, checkout will run through a trusted, secure payment provider. In this preview no payment is collected and no card details are stored.
